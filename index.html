MUSHFIQUR RAHIM

Mohammad Mushfiqur Rahim (Bengali: মুশফিকুর রহিম; born 12 May 1988, alternative DOB: May 09, 1988 (according cricbuzz.com), alternative DOB: June 9, 1987 (according espncricinfo.com) is a Bangladeshi cricketer, and current Test captain for Bangladesh. He is also a former ODI and T20I captain for Bangladesh. He made his Test debut in 2005, at the age of 16, during Bangladesh's first tour of England and the next year played his first One Day International. He specialises as a wicket-keeper and is a middle order batsman. The first Test double centurion for Bangladesh, Rahim is also the most successful wicket-keeper batsman for Bangladesh with most dismissals in Bangladesh history.
